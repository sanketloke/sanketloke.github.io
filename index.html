About

I'm a Senior Applied Scientist at Amazon, and a founding member of the pre-training data team for Amazon Rufus — a large-scale conversational AI shopping assistant powered by frontier LLMs serving hundreds of millions of customers. I co-lead data strategy and training methodology: data curation, scaling laws, training recipes, data mixture/curriculum design, and productionization — shipping 5+ model generations (dense and MoE) with SOTA results on key benchmarks.

My research interests span large language models, foundation model training, data-centric machine learning, agentic mid-training, scaling laws, and representation learning. I have publications at ICLR, NAACL, SIGGRAPH, and IEEE BigData.
