About Me

Hi there! I'm {{ site.author }}, currently a software engineer at Bloomberg. I graduated from Virginia Tech with Masters in Computer Science. As a masters student, I focused on computer vision research in Prof. Jia-Bin's Vision and Learning Lab . I was jointly advised by Prof. Jia-Bin Huang and Prof. Naren Ramakrishnan . My thesis focused on developing a new continual learning method to alleviate catastrophic forgetting in incremental pixel-wise/dense labeling problems.
