About Me

Hi there! I'm {{ site.author }}, currently a masters graduate student at the Virginia Tech, where I'm studying computer science and machine learning.

I have been working on vision research as a member of Jia-Bin's Vision Lab , since Spring 2017. I also work as research assistant in the joint collaboration of Discovery Analytics Center(DAC) and Virginia Tech Transportation Institute (VTTI) on problems at the intersection of computer vision and transportation.

I am jointly advised by Prof. Jia-Bin Huang and Prof. Naren Ramakrishnan . My primary interests are in computer vision and machine learning. Currently, I am focused on improving generalizing capabilities of deep learnt representations trained over different domains.

Experience/Projects

In Spring 2017, I briefly experimented on possible improvements for siamese neural nets based visual tracking in videos with Prof. Jia-Bin Huang.

In Spring 2017, I worked with Prof. Doug Bowman on developing quarterback training environment in a motion-tracked Virtual Reality for American Football. In our user study, we asked questions on how prior experience with football affects the movement and decision-making in VE. [report ] [code ]

In Spring 2017, I was a tutor/teaching assistant for CS 3714: Mobile Software Development at VT.

In Fall 2016, I developed the initial prototype for learning deep agents on half-field offense task in a multi-agent soccer environment. The underlying model used by the agents is DQN with Opponent Modeling from He, He, et al. "Opponent Modeling in Deep Reinforcement Learning". [code ] [For more info, checkout the awesome thesis by Matthew Hausknecht]

In Fall 2016, I collaborated with Yue Cheng , Pratik Anand , Richa Sinha and Prof. Ali.R.Butt , to develop optimizations in Redis targeted for reduction in memory usage for Redis, a key-value store database. [report ] [code ]

During 2015-16, I was a research intern at Indian Institute of Technology,Bombay, where I worked with Prof. Ganesh Ramakrishnan on two projects: natural language processing for taxonomy generation and development of an awesome ecosystem of apps for aggregating knowledge from farmers in rural India!
